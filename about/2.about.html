About US

Mhoub Khmer was recently opened in January 2025. Mhoub Khmer is a sub restaurant by Eleven One Kitchen, Cambodia's first 99% plastic-free restaurant.

Mhoub Khmer came to life through Chef Mongkol’s love for traditional Khmer cuisine and his drive to serve the less fortunate around him. It is this passion that shaped Mhoub Khmer into a training center for young Cambodians. We teach eager students the cooking techniques to send guests on a culinary journey through our traditional Khmer tastes and flavors. Prepare to go back in time as you uncover the secrets of our traditional Khmer cuisine. We’ll deliver an unparalleled dining experience infused with Cambodia’s unique stories and culture.
