About Us

Welcome to MHOUB KHMER

Mhoub Khmer, a sub-restaurant of Eleven One Kitchen, opened in January 2022 as Cambodia’s first 99% plastic-free eatery.

With outlets in Toul Tompong and Boeng Keng Kong 1, it’s a popular choice for expats and locals, offering environmentally friendly Khmer cuisine with local, MSG-free ingredients.

About Our Restaurant

As a proud member of the Restaurant Collection, our goal is straightforward: to offer you an unparalleled dining experience tailored to your tastes. In our establishments, your culinary preferences always take center stage. Through Adventures Cambodia and Akim Vespa, unlock the true essence of Cambodia with tours designed by those deeply rooted in the region. Their expert guides unveil the real Cambodia, featuring an enticing Siem Reap food tour and an exceptional Private Angkor Wat tour. Your journey of a lifetime awaits, complete with a personal driver and the thrill of riding a Vespa scooter or vintage Jeep!


Indulge in Sombai’s array of homemade liqueurs and artisanal products within an artistic Khmer house. Explore a curated shop featuring Siem Reap and Cambodian souvenirs. Sample 10 distinct liqueurs and 3 alcoholic jams – ideal Cambodian gifts and unique Angkor souvenirs for your loved ones. Looking for a cocktail class? They’ve got you covered!
