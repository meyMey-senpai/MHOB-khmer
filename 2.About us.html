Welcome to MHOUB KHMER

Mhoub Khmer, a sub-restaurant of Eleven One Kitchen, opened in January 2022 as Cambodia’s first 99% plastic-free eatery.

With outlets in Toul Svay Prey , it’s a popular choice for expats and locals, offering environmentally friendly Khmer cuisine with local, MSG-free ingredients.
